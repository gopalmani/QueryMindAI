# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in QueryMindAI harassment-free for everyone. Be respectful, assume good intent while addressing impact, accept constructive feedback, and keep technical discussion focused on the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, and sustained disruption. Maintainers may edit, remove, reject, or ban contributions or participants when necessary to protect the community. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers will review reports promptly, protect privacy where possible, and apply proportionate consequences.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
